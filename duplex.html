Duplex apartment


2 bedroom apartment sleeping total 4 people with separate bathrooms. 1st floor consists of fully equipped kitchen, bathroom with WC and bath tub, bedroom with 2 separate beds. 2nd floor has a seating area, master bedroom with double bed and WC and shower separated and a small terrace.

    1st floor kitchen


    The kitchen is fully equipped and the kitchen table is extendable to seat up to 6 people. 

    1st floor kitchen with staircase to 2nd floor


    The open staircase connects the kitchen on the 1st floor with the 2nd floor.

    1st floor bedroom


    The bedroom has two separate 90x200cm beds and a large garderobe (note that in the layout below instead a wide single bed is shown).

    1st floor bathroom


    The bathroom has a bath tub and a WC.

    1st floor layout



    2nd floor seating area


    The seating area has a roof window that can be opened for air to circulate and let in additional light.

    2nd floor terrace


    The terrace is easily accessible via the glass door on the 2nd floor.

    2nd floor bedroom


    The bedroom has a 160cm wide double bed with a clothing stand on each side.

    2nd floor bathroom


    The bathroom which is separate from the shower has a WC with a roof window. 

    2nd floor shower


    The shower which is separate has an electrically operated roof window.
