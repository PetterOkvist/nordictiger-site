Cascais Historical Centre

Two separate apartments in the same building available for short time rentals.


The location is very central in the Cascais historical centre just 200m from the closest beach (Praia da Ribeira, also called Fisherman's beach) and the small city centre of Cascais with the hotel Baia, the Municipal and Citadel buildings. Cascais is easily reachable from Lisbon (ca 30km) or Lisbon airport (ca 35km) via train, car or taxi.


There is a local coffee bar (A Leitaria) with nice small tables outside that serves coffee, biscuits and lighter lunch just 10m away from the apartments.


Both apartments are equipped with high speed internet connection.


Duplex apartment: 2 bedroom apartment sleeping total 4 people with separate bathrooms. 1st floor consists of fully equipped kitchen, bathroom with WC and bath tub, bedroom with 2 separate beds. 2nd floor has a seating area, master bedroom with double bed and WC and shower separated and a small terrace. 


Ground floor apartment: 1 bedroom apartment sleeping 2 people with separate beds, the combined kitchen/living room has a bedsofa that could be used for 2 more people. Bathroom with WC and shower.
